    ICEP (Informatics Community Engagement Programme) is a TUT initiative that connects classroom knowledge with real-world experience. We help Informatics students transition into the industry through hands-on internships, exposing them to the latest technologies, tools, and agile practices. We’re also passionate about giving back — using smart IT solutions to uplift township communities, improve daily living, and support small businesses. At ICEP, we grow talent, solve real problems, and build a better future through tech.

    • Our programme is built around exposing students to real-world IT environments, where they gain hands-on experience with modern technologies, development tools, methodologies, and frameworks.
    • Beyond student development, ICEP is dedicated to driving meaningful change in township communities.
    • ICEP also supports TUT by delivering fast, efficient digital solutions that address operational challenges, all within a structured system governed by clear business rules and best practices.
