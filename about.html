ICEP – Empowering Communities. Enabling Futures.

The Informatics Community Engagement Programme (ICEP) is a strategic initiative by the Department of Informatics at Tshwane University of Technology (TUT). ICEP was created to provide students — who have completed all their theoretical modules — with valuable internship opportunities that bridge the gap between academia and industry.

  • Our programme is built around exposing students to real-world IT environments, where they gain hands-on experience with modern technologies, development tools, methodologies, and frameworks.
  • Beyond student development, ICEP is dedicated to driving meaningful change in township communities.
  • ICEP also supports TUT by delivering fast, efficient digital solutions that address operational challenges, all within a structured system governed by clear business rules and best practices.

We are passionate about creating opportunities, solving real problems, and using technology to make a difference.

Our Vision, Mission & Goal

Vision

To be a leading force in shaping skilled, agile, and innovative ICT professionals who drive technological advancement and create sustainable solutions for the future.

Mission

To equip ICT students with real-world skills and industry experience, bridging the gap between classroom learning and professional success.

Goal

To close the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands through practical engagement and innovation.
